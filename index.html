Personal Projects

WHATSAPP GROUP ANALYSIS:

This work was my final project for the Master's in Data Science and Big Data, graded with the highest mark. I used Python to apply various machine learning techniques, text mining, and NLP for analyzing messages from a WhatsApp group. Specifically, I employed libraries like NLTK for text mining, supervised learning algorithms for dimensionality reduction and clustering of vectorized messages, and also embedding techniques based on pre-trained models like BERT for optimized topic analysis.

SPANISH GOVERNMENT TENDERS ANALYSIS:

With Python, I developed an application for the automatic download of documents containing crucial information from spanish government tenders, such as the application territory or net budget, among others. I transformed the information in XML into structured data, using libraries like polars and pandas for dataframes. From these dataframes, I utilized libraries like Plotly for developing visualizations to present information, such as temporal charts and maps.

AI FOR QUALITATIVE SOCIAL DATA ANALYSIS:

I developed a project aimed at assisting social scientists in conducting qualitative analyses of studies from the CIS (Spanish Center for Sociological Research). For this purpose, I used Python to decompress data and convert interview transcriptions in PDF format into structured data in pandas dataframes. From there, I applied various libraries such as NLTK for text mining and NLP, unsupervised learning techniques like clustering, and pre-trained models like BERT for topic analysis. The goal is to aid in preliminary analyses and provide social scientists with tools that enhance the robustness of conclusions drawn from qualitative analysis.
